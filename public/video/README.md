# A24 Archive Video

Place your A24 film loop video here as: `a24-archive.mp4`

**Recommended specs:**
- Format: MP4 (H.264)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- Duration: 10-30 seconds
- File size: < 10MB (compressed)
- Framerate: 24fps or 30fps
- No audio (will be muted)

**Suggested content:**
- Montage of iconic A24 scenes
- Single continuous shot from an A24 film
- Abstract/atmospheric footage from A24 catalog
